Capacitybay focus on preparing individuals and groups for in-demand
jobs in information technology. We also work with businesses,
nonprofit organizations and government agencies in leading
and managing apprenticeship efforts and activities for young
people and students, dislocated workers and others, preparing
them for rewarding careers and employment in information technology.
At CapacityBay, Inc., we embrace and foster the following core values
which also values reflect and support our mission: Hard work, Punctuality,
Respect, Cooperation and collaboration, Partnership, Diversity, Tolerance,
Teamwork, Research, Equity.
